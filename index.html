Professional Career

Prof. (Dr.) Nagesh Kallappa Chougule is presently serving as the Head of the Mechanical Engineering Department at COEP Technological University, Pune, India (COEP Tech). He boasts approximately 34 years of experience in teaching and consulting in the fields of CAD/CAM/CAE and Thermal Engineering. Additionally, he oversees the CAD/CAM/CAE & Optimisation center and Renewable Energy Lab. Prof. Chougule holds a PhD in Mechanical Engineering and has authored three textbooks while also developing numerous mechanical engineering application softwares. He has visited several international universities and has published a considerable number of papers at both national and international levels.


For many years, he has been conducting various certificate courses on CAD/CAM/CAE and PLM software for academicians and industry professionals under the Continuing Education Program (CEP) of the institute. Furthermore, he possesses significant experience in consultancy regarding structural, thermal, and fluid flow simulation problems, as well as design verification for industry and government projects. Recently, Prof. Chougule established a Centre of Excellence for bamboo utilization in daily life applications at COEP. He leads research projects in the field of sustainability, focusing on the utilization of bamboo and biomass pellets for energy generation at the household level, as well as promoting clean cookstove design.


Prof. Chougule also serves as an expert for NBA accreditation visits and has conducted evaluations in approximately 100 institutes across India. He holds life membership in professional bodies such as The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) and the International Association of Engineers (IAENG). Additionally, Prof. Chougule has worked as a member of the Board of Studies (BOS) in various universities and has delivered numerous expert sessions.
